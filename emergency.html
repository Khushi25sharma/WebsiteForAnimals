First Aid Tips

  • Bleeding: Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth. Seek vet help if severe.
  • Choking: Check mouth for objects. If not breathing, perform gentle chest compressions.
  • Heatstroke: Move pet to shade, offer water, cool with damp towels. Call vet.
  • Poisoning: Do not induce vomiting. Call your vet or poison helpline immediately.
  • Fractures: Keep pet still, support injured area, transport to vet carefully.
