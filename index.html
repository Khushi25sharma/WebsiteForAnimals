Our Vision & Mission

At Paws & Care, we envision a world where every animal is safe, loved, and respected. Our mission is to rescue, heal, and rehome animals in need, while educating and empowering our community to be compassionate advocates for all creatures.

Rescue

We save abandoned, injured, and abused animals, providing them with shelter and medical care.

Rehabilitate

Our team nurtures and rehabilitates animals, helping them recover physically and emotionally.

Rehome

We find loving forever homes for our rescues, ensuring a happy new beginning for every animal.

“Every animal deserves a chance at happiness. Together, we can make it happen.”
— Dr. Meera Kapoor, Founder
