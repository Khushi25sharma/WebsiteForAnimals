About Paws & Care

Dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals and their human companions.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive care and support for animals in need, ensuring they find loving homes and receive the best possible veterinary care.

What We Do

From rescue and rehabilitation to adoption services and veterinary care, we're committed to improving animal welfare in our community.

Our Impact

Over 1,000 animals rescued and rehomed, providing essential care and support to countless pets and their families.
